About Ify Makes And More

Our Story

Founded with a passion for bringing quality and joy to everyday life, Ify Makes And More started as a small venture dedicated to creating delicious homemade treats and has grown into a comprehensive lifestyle brand.

From our signature chin chin and refreshing zobo drinks to premium perfumes, stylish men's fashion, and comfortable accommodation, we're committed to excellence in everything we do.

Quality First

Every product is crafted with care, attention to detail, and a commitment to excellence

Our Mission

To provide exceptional products and services that bring joy and satisfaction to our customers' lives.

Our Values

Quality, integrity, customer satisfaction, and continuous innovation in everything we do.

Our Vision

To become the leading lifestyle brand known for quality products and exceptional customer experience.
